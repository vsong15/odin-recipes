Jajangmyeon

Description

Jajangmyeon is a popular Korean black bean noodle dish that features thick udon noodles coated in savory black bean sauce.

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles - 200 grams
  • Pork belly - 150 grams
  • Zucchini - 1/2 a whole
  • Potato - 1/2 a whole
  • Onion - 1/2 a whole
  • Black bean paste (chunjang) - 4 tablespoons
  • Cornstarch - 1 tablespoon
  • Sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • Water - 2 cups
  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - a pinch

Steps

  1. Cook the udon noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Thinly slice the pork belly, zucchini, potato, and onion.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the black bean paste, cornstarch, sugar, and water to make the jajang sauce.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  5. Add the sliced pork belly and cook until browned.
  6. Add the sliced vegetables (zucchini, potato, and onion) and stir-fry for a few minutes until slightly softened.
  7. Pour the jajang sauce into the skillet or wok and stir well to combine.
  8. Cook for a few more minutes until the sauce thickens.
  9. Divide the cooked udon noodles into bowls and top with the jajang sauce.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
